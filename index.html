Research Interests

My research is focused on theoretical machine learning with the broad goal of understanding the asymptotic of learning in deep neural networks. I apply tools from high-dimensional statistics and large-scale optimization to provide rigorous theoretical guarantees for learning and generalization in machine learning problems. I am also interested in high-dimensional time series models with applications to computational neuroscience such as modeling complex neural sensory responses in the brain.
